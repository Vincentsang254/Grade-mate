Privacy Policy — Grade Mate

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Grade Mate is a student-focused GPA calculator and academic tracker. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Grade Mate does not collect personal information.

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Courses (course name, credit hours, grades)
  • Exams (exam name, date/time, optional location)
  • App settings (GPA scale, grade format, preferences)
  • Optional local backup data

All data is stored locally using device storage and is not transmitted to any external server.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate your GPA
  • To display your courses and academic statistics
  • To manage your exam schedule
  • To allow backup and restore features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data.

Grade Mate does not include analytics or advertising SDKs.

4. Data Control

You are in full control of your data. You can:

  • Edit or delete individual courses or exams
  • Delete all stored data
  • Reset the app from settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

5. Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend using device-level security such as PIN, password, or biometrics to protect your information.
