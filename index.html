Privacy Policy for Grade Mate

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Grade Mate is a student focused GPA calculator and academic tracker. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Grade Mate does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Courses (course name, credit hours, grades)
  • Exams (exam name, date/time)

All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted, uploaded, or shared with any external servers or third parties.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate your GPA
  • To display your courses and academic statistics
  • To manage your exam schedule

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data.

Grade Mate does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

4. Data Control

You are in full control of your data. You can:

  • Edit or delete individual courses or exams
  • Delete all stored data
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

5. Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend using device-level security such as a PIN, password, or biometrics to protect your information.

6. Children’s Privacy

Grade Mate is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the app’s store listing with an updated date.
