Privacy Policy for Grade Mate

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Grade Mate is a student focused GPA calculator and exam tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Grade Mate does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal or sensitive user data.

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Courses (course name, credit hours, grades)
  • Exams (exam name, date and time)

All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted, uploaded, or shared with any external servers or third parties.

Grade Mate does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any user data over the internet.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate your GPA
  • To display your courses and academic statistics
  • To manage your exam schedule

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data.

Grade Mate does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking technologies.

4. Permissions

Grade Mate does not require any sensitive device permissions. If any permissions are requested by the system, they are used only for core app functionality such as storing data locally on your device.

No permissions are used to collect, access, or share personal data.

5. Data Control

You are in full control of your data. You can:

  • Edit or delete individual courses or exams
  • Delete all stored data within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

6. Children’s Privacy

Grade Mate is intended for general student use and does not specifically target children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the app’s store listing with an updated date.
