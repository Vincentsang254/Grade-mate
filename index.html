Privacy Policy

Grade Mate (Student GPA) — course & exam tracker with GPA calculator

Effective: 2026-02-21 App name: Grade Mate Platform: Android

Grade Mate is designed to help students track courses, credit hours, grades, and upcoming exams. Your data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (SharedPreferences).

This page is written to be clear and practical. If you need a version for a specific store requirement, you can update the contact and any optional sections.

1) Summary of our values

  • Privacy first: we minimize the data we collect.
  • Local by default: your courses and exams are stored on your device.
  • Control: you can delete courses/exams and reset all data at any time.
  • Transparency: we explain what features do, including notifications.

2) Intended users

Grade Mate is intended for:

  • High school, college, and university students
  • Learners who want a simple GPA calculator and course list
  • Students who want exam reminders and a lightweight planner

3) Core features

  • Course management: add, edit, delete courses with credit hours and grades.
  • GPA calculation: automatic GPA computation based on total grade points and credits.
  • Exam scheduling: add exams with date/time and view upcoming/past/today exams.
  • Search & filtering: quickly find courses or exams by name.
  • Backup & restore (local): create a backup snapshot and restore it later (stored on-device).
  • Settings: manage preferences such as reminders and other app options.
  • Notifications (optional): exam reminders, only if enabled by you.

4) Data we store

Grade Mate stores the following types of data locally on your device:

  • Courses: course name, credit hours, grade value/letter mapping.
  • Exams: exam name, exam date/time (and optional location if you add one).
  • App settings: GPA scale/format, reminder days, and other preferences you set.
  • Backups: if you use backup/restore, a backup JSON is saved locally.

5) Data we collect

Grade Mate does not require you to create an account and does not ask for personal identifiers like your name, email, phone number, or student ID.

If you later add features like cloud sync, analytics, or ads, you should update this policy accordingly.

6) Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app can schedule local exam reminders on your device using flutter_local_notifications. Notification content typically includes the exam name and an “Exam Reminder” message.

  • You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings and/or in-app settings.
  • Scheduled reminders are local; they do not require a server.
  • Exact scheduling may vary depending on Android battery optimizations and permission settings.

7) Sharing your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Grade Mate does not share your course/exam records with third parties because they are stored locally on your device.

8) Data retention & deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it. You can:

  • Delete individual courses or exams
  • Delete all courses or all exams
  • Reset all data from the settings screen
  • Uninstall the app to remove the app’s local storage

9) Security

We use standard on-device storage provided by the platform. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we aim to keep the app simple and minimize exposure by not transmitting your data.

10) Children’s privacy

Grade Mate is an educational utility app. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can help. The app does not require personal information to function.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or new features. When we do, we will update the “Effective” date at the top of this page.

Long description (store listing)

Grade Mate — Student GPA & Exam Planner

Grade Mate helps you stay on top of your academic progress by keeping your courses, grades, credit hours, and exams organized in one clean app. Add courses, calculate your GPA instantly, and schedule exam dates so you’re never caught off guard.

What you can do

  • Add & manage courses: save course names, credit hours, and grades.
  • Automatic GPA calculation: see your current GPA update as you edit your courses.
  • Exam scheduling: add upcoming exams with date & time and track what’s next.
  • Search & quick access: find a course or exam fast when your list grows.
  • Optional reminders: enable exam notifications so you don’t miss important dates.
  • Backup & restore (local): save a local backup and restore when needed.

Who it’s for

  • Students who want a simple and reliable GPA calculator
  • Students tracking multiple courses and credit hours
  • Learners who want a lightweight exam planner with reminders

Your data, your control

Grade Mate stores your courses and exams locally on your device. You can delete individual items, clear everything, or reset the app anytime from settings.
